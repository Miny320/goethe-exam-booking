Language skill level B2 Goethe-Zertifikat B2

About the exam

Location

Goethe-Institut / Max Mueller Bhavan, Park Mansions, Gate 4, 57A, Park Street, Kolkata - 700016, India

Exam date

23.09.2025

Please reserve this period. The exact details on time and date will be sent to you by email approximately 3 weeks before the examination date.

Parts of the exam

The exam consists of four modules:
Listening 40 minutes
Reading 65 minutes
Writing 75 minutes
Speaking 15 minutes

Prerequisites for participation

recommended from 16 years of age

Minimum number of participants

The minimum number of participants is 10. If the minimum number of participants is not reached, the examination will not take place as a consequence. In such a case, we will do our best to offer you an alternative in good time. If you do not want to accept the suggested alternative, any payments already made will be promptly refunded.

Rebate for course participants
If you have attended a course at a Goethe-Institut in the last 6 months, you will be able to book the exam at a price of INR 16,000.00*. A single module costs INR 4,000.00*. The 6 months refer to the period between the end of the course and the exam date. The price is automatically reduced at booking.

incl. 18% GST
